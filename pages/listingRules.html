Listing Rules

We have a strict rules in place to maintain a level playing field for our community.

Only list your own deals or on behalf of the sourcers you actually represent

It is strictly forbidden to publish deals without knowledge and consent of their owner.

Only include information relevant to the specific deal

Don't place any contact information in description area. Use designated boxes as intended.

Only use pictures relevant to the property in your listings.

Don't use any kind of graphics with your advertisement as a picture

Pictures should be plain, without any additional information indicating company name or contact details

Failing to complying with our rules will affect you Aquila Reputation and might result in suspending or banning your account

Each listing is reviewed manually to ensure it complies with our terms of use, it might take up to 48 hours to approve it
